About Me

Allow me to tell you a little bit about myself...

BIO

Hello there! My name is Eric Nunn. I am a Front-End Web Developer and current Owner/Founder of Lowly Hero Web Development & Technology, Co. I specialize in creating mobile-responsive, user-friendly websites using technologies such as:

  • HTML
  • CSS/Sass
  • Bootstrap
  • JavaScript
  • jQuery
  • Node.js and npm
  • Git/Git Bash
  • Gulp
  • Chrome DevTools

I love taking things apart and putting them back together again in order to figure out what makes things work! This passion for understanding how the world works "underneath the hood" eventually led me to explore how and why our world was being rapidly dominated by these little boxes located in homes across the world. This spark of curiosity turned into an obsession with computer programming. I quickly realized that to be able to communicate with a computer was to wield a tremendous power. The first time I went on Codecademy and made "Hello World!" show up in a browser, I was hooked. My keyboard suddenly became my wand, and my computer became my book of spells. Now roughly 2 years after that first "Hello World" showed up on my screen, my goal is to find a Front-End Developer role with a company that shares my passion for creating beautiful, functional user-experiences.
